Christmas Festival

Christmas is an annual festival commemorating the birth of Jesus Christ, observed primarily on December 25th as a religious and cultural celebration among billions of people around the world.

The traditional Christmas narrative recounts that Jesus was born in Bethlehem, in accordance with messianic prophecies. When Joseph and Mary arrived in the city, they were offered a stable where the Christ Child was born.

Popular modern customs include gift giving, Christmas music and caroling, church services, special meals, and the display of Christmas decorations including trees, lights, and nativity scenes.
