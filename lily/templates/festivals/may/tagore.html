Rabindranath Tagore Jayanti

Celebrating the Birth Anniversary of the Bard of Bengal

RABINDRANATH TAGORE JAYANTI

Rabindra Jayanti is an annually celebrated cultural festival, prevalent among Tagorephiles around the world, commemorating the birth anniversary of Rabindranath Tagore. Celebrated on the 25th day of the Bengali month of Boishakh (typically in early May), this day marks the birth of the Nobel laureate in 1861.

The celebrations feature various cultural programs including performances of Tagore's songs (Rabindra Sangeet), poetry recitations, dance dramas, and theatrical productions of his works. Educational institutions across Bengal organize special events, while global communities of Tagore enthusiasts host commemorative gatherings.

Tagore's contributions to literature, music, and art are remembered through academic seminars and exhibitions. The day serves as a tribute to his legacy in shaping Bengali culture and his progressive ideas on education and internationalism. Traditional celebrations include decorating venues with alpona patterns and reading from Tagore's extensive body of work.

Celebrated annually on 25th Boishakh (May 7-9 in Gregorian calendar)
